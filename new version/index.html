A Story of a Lifechange

Let me tell You a story of the lifechange project I made not to regret ever again I hadn't done it sooner

Description

I'm a newbie in this vast IT-ocean. I fell into the water, I'm in the middle of nowhere, I don't know what I'm doing. But as long as I'm floating and seeing the horizon, I know I finally figure out all of this.

Explanation

My code is to me like a little brother or little sister - a bit annoying, a bit messy, sometimes unpredictive, sometimes repetitive, but in the end of the day You love Your younger sibling anyway, don't You?

Motivation

To have fun, to understand modern world, to channel creativity, to have better career oportunities. But also to inspire others, especially those who hesitate or have little faith in themselves.

Realization

Pharaphrasing a wise man: it's good to have strength to change what can be changed, humility to accept unchangeable and reason to always tell the difference. When You have all of these, You just need some tools.
