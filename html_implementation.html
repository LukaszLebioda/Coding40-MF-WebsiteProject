Implementation

A simple story of a complex project

How a sales program was implemented

where there was none before

Background

Success?

This is a story of a software implementation. Carried out with passion and devotion. With no previous experience of people involved. And no plan whatsoever.

The description below doesn't fully reflect all the effort and stress and difficulties related to the implementation progress. That was quite an adventure... I would be honored if You take a look.

Pre-implementation

We need a software!

Once upon a time there was a popular museum in a big city with no integrated software to manage bookings, sell souvenirs & tickets online or at the ticket office, rent conference rooms, manage employees working hours...

A change needed to be done to make lifes of both Employees an Clients easier. Some sort of sales & booking program would be a breakthgrough presumably. But where to start?

Human resources

And we need people!

No one really knew how to do this, me included. Yet I became the one who led and coordinated all that huge effort from the very beggining till the very end.

With the support of friendly people, both co-workers and contractors, with multiple stomach twists and nails bitten, and hair that turned grey, it was finally over.

Research

What kind of sotware?

First we did some research to recognize the needs of both Clients and Employees. We wrote those requirements down one by one and send them to some companies we found online.

The we looked through all the responses and decided wich one best suits our expectations. What was left was to buy a product according to every legal requirements, sing up a contract and wait for the first software samples to come.

Testing, training

How does it work?

Softare finally come and the whole team needed to adapt quickly, as the expectations grew high. The process of testing and learning started.

At first most needed modules - administration, ticket booking, room rental, personnel management - were launched and soon all the data migrated from testing to production. We started working with the software for real.

Website

We want to sell online

Next step was to make online sale possible. First we built a website on our own but soon it occured a professional one is required.

The whole process of requirements' research, design company selection, sales programm integration, testing and training started over again. But finally the website was ready to launch. Both websites are to be seen: here.

Payment operator

We want to sell online

Simultaneously the process of payment operator selection started. The whole process of requirements' research, design company selection, sales programm integration, testing and training started over again.

It was complicated as we needed both online operator as well as payment terminal operator. But finally we did it and the website was launched.

Maintenance

The software needs to be taken care of

The job was never done. Software needed to be maintained (via technical support), people needed to be trained and their permissions needed to be specified.
